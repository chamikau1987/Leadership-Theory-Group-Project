Kyoto College Of Graduate Studies For Informatics

What Is KCGI

  1. the Kyoto college of graduate studies for informatics inherits the tradition and achievement of japans first computer education institution. Being japans first computer education institution and through its tradition and achievements, Kyoto computer Gakuin has been training information processing engineers to meet the needs of industry for 59 years and counting. In addition, based on the global education network with the Rochester Institute of Technology and their overseas universities. KCGI incorporates the world’s latest IT education curriculum.KCGI includes the management and business education aims to train highly skilled professionals in the IT field fostering top leaders in IT applications fields such as CIOs in a way that conventional graduate schools of research have not been able to.


  2. The kcg.edu is the only college in Japan which gives graduate school in English medium for international students. Kcgi has four groups.
    • 1. The Kyoto college of graduate studies for informatics.
    • 2. kyoto computer gakuin.
    • 3. Kyoto computer Gakuin automobiles school.
    • 4. kyoto Japanese language training center.
  3. The Kyoto college of graduate studies for informatics will respond to the needs of society, take charge of the times and train applied information technology specialists with advanced practical abilities and creativity to lead the next generation. In recent years with the progress of science and technology, remarkable technological innovations and rapid changes in the social economy. There are growing expectations for the development of advanced professionals in the IT fields who are active socially and at an international level. In response to such a challenge, the Kyoto college of graduate studies for informatics was established as japans first and only graduate school specializing in IT in April 2004.


  4. Why do international students choose the KCGI?

    The KCGI is Japan's only college that accepts international students and gives learning portals in the English medium. It has a reasonable college fee. It has a good scholarship facility for genuine students and has Japanese language class opportunities, job hunting, and career counseling facilities. There are available multiple subjects which provide a wide platform for all students to choose their interest subjects. Being located this college in the heart of the city there is a good facility of transportation to go-to destination. In the college, there are available teachers from different countries like Nepal, Sri Lanka, Ukraine, the USA, and China, that help the respective international students with any kind of problem to counsel in their native language.


  5. Course study of KCGI

    KCGI is a college of IT. The courses are in a technical field related to IT and computers. As Japan is a country of technology. All the areas including the official and nonofficial IT are necessary. The main motive of this college is to teach the subject related to IT. Subjects like Web page development, Networking, IT in tourism, etc are taught in this college. This college allows international students to get an education in the English medium. The student gets job opportunities after graduating from this college.
